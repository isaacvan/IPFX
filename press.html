Press & Media

IPFX Capital is a UK-based fintech startup preparing to launch a new, algorithm-driven proprietary trading funding model in March 2026. The company aims to address structural issues in the prop-trading industry by replacing pay-to-play challenges and rigid rule-sets with data-led capital allocation based on real trading behaviour.

What IPFX Capital Is Building

Launching March 2026

Infinity Challenge

A free, multi-stage trading challenge designed to pay traders as they scale through increasing account sizes. Capital allocation and post-challenge funding are informed by algorithmic analysis of trading performance, consistency, and risk management.

Planned

PAC Challenge

A direct capital allocation pathway where traders submit verified trading history. IPFX's algorithm assesses strategy characteristics and risk appetite to determine a suitable account size.

Planned

Trading Pool

A model allowing experienced traders to monetise their strategies by making them available within a shared trading pool, with compensation designed to exceed traditional prop-firm payouts.

Why This Matters

Most prop firms rely on upfront fees and evaluation models where the majority of traders fail without compensation. IPFX Capital is exploring an alternative approach focused on:

  • Reducing pay-to-fail incentives
  • Rewarding consistency rather than short-term risk
  • Using data, not arbitrary rules, to allocate capital

Founders

IPFX Capital was founded by UK-based entrepreneurs with backgrounds in trading, technology, and quantitative analysis. The founding team is focused on long-term alignment between traders and capital providers.
