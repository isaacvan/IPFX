Frequently Asked Questions
How do I withdraw my profits?

Go to Payouts in your dashboard, enter the amount you want to withdraw and submit. You keep 90% of all profits. Payouts are processed within 1–3 business days to your connected payment method. You must have passed the consistency check to be eligible.

What happens if I breach my drawdown limit?

Your account is suspended automatically. For Infinity and Traditional accounts you can reset or reapply. For Application Challenge accounts, a breach of your agreed parameters ends the contract — you can reapply with a new entry fee.

Can I trade any instrument?

Infinity and Traditional accounts support forex, indices, commodities and crypto (platform dependent). Application Challenge accounts are restricted to the instruments agreed during your parameter negotiation — this is part of what protects both you and IPFX Capital.

How is the Application Challenge different from Infinity?

Infinity is an evaluation programme — you pass phases to get funded. The Application Challenge has no evaluation: you disclose your strategy, our analysts backtest it, agree custom parameters with you, and you are funded from day one. It is designed for traders with a proven, systematic edge.

Can I use an Expert Advisor (EA) or automated strategy?

Yes — EAs and algorithmic strategies are fully permitted and encouraged on all IPFX accounts. For the Application Challenge, your algorithm logic must be disclosed during the application so our analysts can backtest and set appropriate parameters.

How do I scale my capital beyond my current account?

For Infinity accounts, consistent performance over 3+ months qualifies you to apply for a larger account up to £200K. For Application Challenge traders, after an agreed review period demonstrating consistent edge, you can apply for capital scaling up to £500K at no additional fee.

What trading platforms does IPFX support?

IPFX Capital supports MetaTrader 4 (MT4), MetaTrader 5 (MT5) and cTrader. Choose your platform when setting up your account. Application Challenge traders agree their platform during the parameter negotiation phase.
