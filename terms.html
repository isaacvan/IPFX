4.6 Infinity Challenge — Detailed Programme Rules

The Infinity Challenge is a four-stage skill evaluation programme. Entry is free of charge. The following rules are exact, mandatory, and non-negotiable. Breach of any rule at any stage will result in the outcome described in clause 4.6.5. These rules are reproduced here in full so that no Participant can dispute whether they were made aware of them.

By activating an Infinity Challenge account, the Participant confirms they have read, understood, and accepted every rule set out in clauses 4.6.1 through 4.6.6 in full.

4.6.1 Stage 1 — Evaluation ($1,000 Virtual Capital)

Stage 1 is the initial skill filter. The Participant trades a simulated $1,000 account subject to all of the following rules simultaneously — all rules apply at all times and there are no exceptions:

  • Profit Target: The Participant must achieve a minimum net profit of 10% ($100) to complete Stage 1. Profit is calculated on closed trades only.
  • Daily Loss Limit: The Participant must not allow their account equity or balance to fall by more than 3% ($30) in any single calendar day (00:00–23:59 server time). Reaching this limit on any day constitutes an immediate breach regardless of overall account performance.
  • Trailing Drawdown Limit: The Participant must not allow their account to sustain a trailing drawdown exceeding 8% ($80). The trailing drawdown is measured from the account's all-time equity peak. Once the drawdown reaches $80 below the highest equity point ever recorded, the account is in breach.
  • Minimum Trading Days: The Participant must complete a minimum of 10 active trading days. A trading day is defined as a calendar day on which at least one trade has been both opened and closed. Days on which only positions are held open (without a new open-and-close cycle) do not count.
  • Minimum Trade Count: The Participant must execute a minimum of 20 completed round-trip trades across the evaluation period.
  • Maximum Risk per Trade: No single trade may risk more than 1% of the account balance ($10) at the time of entry. Risk is measured as the maximum potential loss on the position at the defined stop-loss level, or, where no stop-loss is set, as the full notional value of the position relative to account balance as determined by the Company.
  • Daily Profit Cap: The Participant must not realise more than 3% ($30) net profit within any single trading day. Profits in excess of this cap on any individual day will not count toward the profit target and may, at the Company's discretion, be deducted from the Participant's running total if the cap is deliberately and repeatedly exploited.
  • Maximum Attempts: A Participant may attempt Stage 1 a maximum of three (3) times per calendar month at no cost. A fourth attempt in the same calendar month is not permitted; the Participant must wait until the first day of the following calendar month.

4.6.2 Stage 2 — Funded Simulation ($5,000 Simulated Capital)

On passing Stage 1, the Participant is promoted to Stage 2 and granted access to a $5,000 simulated funded account. Stage 2 eliminates luck-based performers: the consistency and minimum-day requirements are designed to ensure only traders with repeatable edge progress. All rules below apply simultaneously:

  • Profit Target: 10% net profit ($500) on closed trades.
  • Profit Split: 90% of net profits generated in Stage 2 accrue to the Participant. These earnings are held and are not disbursed at Stage 2 completion; they are released when the Participant reaches the 3% milestone in Stage 3 (see clause 4.6.3).
  • Daily Loss Limit: 3% ($150) per calendar day. Breach on any single day is an immediate rule breach.
  • Trailing Drawdown Limit: 6% ($300) trailing from all-time equity peak. Reaching this limit closes the account immediately.
  • Minimum Trading Days: 15 active trading days (as defined in clause 4.6.1) must be completed before the profit target is considered achieved.
  • Profitable Days Requirement: A minimum of 60% of the Participant's total trading days in Stage 2 must be profitable (net positive closed P&L on that day). Failing to maintain 60% profitable days means the profit target cannot be confirmed even if the monetary target has been reached.
  • Maximum Risk per Trade: 1% of account balance ($50) per trade.
  • Payout Timing: Stage 2 earnings are held in full until the Participant reaches the 3% milestone in Stage 3. There is no Stage 2 payout at the time of Stage 2 completion.

4.6.3 Stage 3 — Qualification ($10,000 Simulated Capital)

Stage 3 is the final simulated evaluation stage. The Participant's full trading history from all prior stages is reviewed by the Company's quantitative team upon completion. All rules below apply simultaneously:

  • Full Profit Target: 10% net profit ($1,000) on closed trades.
  • Early Milestone — 3% ($300): The moment the Participant's closed net profit reaches 3% ($300) in Stage 3, the Stage 2 earnings are automatically released as the Participant's first payout. This does not complete Stage 3; the full 10% target remains outstanding.
  • Stage 3 Payout: On successful completion of Stage 3 (reaching $1,000 net profit), the Participant receives a second payout equal to 90% of their net Stage 3 profits.
  • Daily Loss Limit: 2.5% ($250) per calendar day. Breach is immediate termination from Stage 3, and the Participant returns to Stage 1.
  • Maximum Risk per Trade: 1% of account balance ($100) per trade.
  • Minimum Trading Days: 10 active trading days (as defined in clause 4.6.1).
  • Quant Review: All trades across Stages 1, 2, and 3 are reviewed by the Company's quantitative analysts upon Stage 3 completion. The review assesses: Sharpe ratio, maximum drawdown behaviour, average risk-to-reward per trade, position sizing consistency, session and instrument concentration, and whether profitability reflects genuine edge or statistical noise. The Company reserves the right to decline Stage 4 entry to any Participant who passed the numerical targets but whose trading pattern does not, in the Company's reasonable judgement, reflect systematic, repeatable trading skill.
  • Review Timeframe: The quant review will be completed within 24 hours of Stage 3 completion. The Participant will be notified of the outcome.

4.6.4 Stage 4 — Professional Funded Account (Live / A-Book)

Participants who pass the quant review are allocated a Stage 4 professional funded account. Stage 4 operates on real markets. The following terms apply:

  • Capital Allocation: Stage 4 capital is determined by the Company's analysts in the range of $25,000 to $500,000 based on the Participant's Stage 3 metrics. The allocation is final and is not negotiable. No Participant has a contractual right to any specific allocation amount.
  • Profit Split: 90% of all net realised profits accrue to the Participant, paid bi-weekly subject to the conditions in Section 10.
  • Scaling: The Company may increase a Stage 4 Participant's capital allocation over time based on Sharpe ratio, drawdown behaviour, and risk-to-reward metrics, entirely at the Company's discretion. No Participant has any contractual entitlement to a capital increase.
  • Trading Parameters: All drawdown, daily loss, and position sizing limits applicable in Stage 3 remain in force on Stage 4 unless expressly modified in writing by the Company. Additional risk controls may be applied at any time.
  • Real Markets: Stage 4 accounts operate on live market infrastructure. The risks associated with real-market trading (slippage, liquidity, gap risk) apply in full. The Company accepts no liability for losses arising from these conditions.
