Common Questions

Everything you need to know about the Infinity Challenge

Is it really 100% free to start? +

Yes, absolutely. No payment required, no credit card needed, no hidden fees. You receive instant access to a £1,000 evaluation account. The only investment required is your time and trading skill.

When do I start receiving payouts? +

Payouts begin once you reach Phase 3 (A-book funded status). Your Phase 2 profits are tracked and added to your Phase 3 account balance. Once in Phase 3, you receive weekly payouts on your trading profits.

What happens if I fail a phase? +

You can restart immediately with unlimited attempts. If you violate rules in Phase 1, simply restart Phase 1. If you fail in Phase 2 or 3, you'll restart from Phase 1. This ensures consistent performance across all levels and protects both parties.

How is this business model sustainable? +

Unlike firms that profit from evaluation fees, we generate revenue from Phase 3 trading volume and market spreads. It's slower to become profitable as a business, but it ensures we're genuinely invested in your long-term success. Quality traders who reach Phase 3 become long-term revenue sources for both parties.

Can I do both Infinity and Traditional challenges? +

Yes. You can maintain one Infinity Challenge account while also purchasing Traditional Challenge accounts. Many traders use Infinity for skill development and learning while using Traditional for faster access to larger capital.

Which trading platforms are supported? +

We provide access to our proprietary trading platform (web-based and desktop application). It supports all major forex pairs, indices, commodities, and cryptocurrencies. Additional platforms may be added based on trader feedback and demand.
