Trading Rules & Requirements

Transparent rules for sustainable trading success:

  • Daily loss limit: Maximum 5% daily drawdown from starting balance
  • Total loss limit: Maximum 10% total drawdown from starting balance
  • Minimum trading days: 10 active trading days before first payout
  • Profit consistency: No single day exceeds 40% of total profits
  • Allowed instruments: Forex, Indices, Commodities, Cryptocurrencies
  • Trading style: All strategies allowed (scalping, day trading, swing trading)
  • Overnight and weekend positions permitted
  • Expert Advisors (EAs) and copy trading permitted

Scaling Your Account

Grow your capital and profit share over time

How Account Scaling Works

After achieving your profit target and demonstrating consistent profitability over minimum 3 months, you qualify for account scaling. Requirements for each tier:

  • Minimum 3 consecutive profitable months
  • Zero breaches of daily or total loss limits
  • Consistent activity (minimum 40 trading days per evaluation period)
  • Positive risk-reward ratios maintained throughout period
  • Maximum 7% drawdown in any single month

Meeting these criteria qualifies your account for the next tier with increased capital and improved profit splits. Scaling typically occurs every 3-6 months based on performance.
