    Transparency

    How we operate — openly.

    Where your fee goes

    Challenge fees fund the risk pool used to back real funded accounts. A portion covers platform and operational costs. We don't take fees and disappear — they underwrite the capital you're trading with.

    How payouts work

    Payouts are processed bi-weekly. Once your request is submitted in the dashboard, it is reviewed and issued within the standard cycle. No manufactured delays. We publish our payout timeline clearly in the dashboard.

    Account protection

    Your funded account operates within the loss limits you agreed to at the start. We do not move the drawdown limit mid-challenge. What you signed up for is what you get — in writing, in your dashboard, no changes.

    What happens if you fail

    A failed challenge is a failed challenge — not a ban, not a blacklist. You can re-enter immediately, often at a discounted rate. We want good traders to succeed, and setbacks are part of trading.
