Trading Rules & Requirements

Simple, transparent rules designed for your success:

  • Daily loss limit: Maximum 5% daily drawdown from starting balance
  • Total loss limit: Maximum 10% total drawdown from starting balance
  • Minimum trading days: 10 active trading days before first payout
  • Profit consistency: No single day should represent more than 40% of total profits
  • Allowed instruments: Forex, Indices, Commodities, Cryptocurrencies
  • Trading style: All strategies allowed (scalping, day trading, swing trading)
  • No restrictions on holding trades overnight or over weekends
  • Expert Advisors (EAs) and copy trading permitted

Scaling Your Account

Grow your capital and profit share

How Account Scaling Works

After successfully completing your profit target and demonstrating consistent profitability over at least 3 months, you become eligible for account scaling. Each scaling milestone requires:

  • Minimum 3 consecutive profitable months
  • No breaches of daily or total loss limits
  • Consistent trading activity (minimum 40 trading days per evaluation period)
  • Positive risk-reward ratios maintained
  • Maximum drawdown not exceeding 7% in any single month

When you meet these criteria, your account automatically qualifies for the next tier with increased capital and improved profit splits.
