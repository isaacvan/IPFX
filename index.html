What is futures trading? Futures are contracts to buy or sell a financial asset (like the S&P 500, Nasdaq, Crude Oil, or Gold) at a set price on a future date. Unlike forex, you trade standardised CME Group exchange-listed contracts — ES (S&P 500 E-mini), NQ (Nasdaq E-mini), CL (Crude Oil), GC (Gold) — and their micro equivalents (1/10th contract size: MES, MNQ, MCL, MGC). Platform: IPFX Markets. Micro contracts available on all account sizes. EOD trailing drawdown — no intraday drawdown breaches. Weekly payouts from day one of funding.
